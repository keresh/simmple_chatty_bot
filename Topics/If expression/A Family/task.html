A Family

Annie has a family: mother Lucy, father James, sister Emily, and brother Luke. She creates a family tree, that you see below.

Now she wants to make this tree interactive. First, she wants the program to be able to determine the relationship between her and a random relative. For this, the program can use the information about the relative's parents (compare two people's parents), and if that person is male or female.

How many if-else expressions will she need to use to find out the relationship between Annie and her random relative?
